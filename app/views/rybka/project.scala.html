Project
Basic idea of “Rybka Project” is to research proteins responsible for memory consolidation, reconsolidation, and learning, as well as their expressions with Zebra-fish model.
The basic idea is that if we manage to make those proteins glow and the fish will be transparent we will be able to watch them glowing while and after fish learns something while solving some cognitive tasks
What we need:
Transparent strain of zebra-fish called Casper
Cell culture
Vectors to deliver genetic modifications
Aquarium + water + food + care about zebra-fishes
Videocamera + code to track fish movement and glowing
Protocols and papers
